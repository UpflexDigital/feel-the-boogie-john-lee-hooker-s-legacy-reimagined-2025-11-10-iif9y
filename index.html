About Boogie Legacy

Our mission is to preserve and celebrate the legendary legacy of John Lee Hooker for generations to come.

Our Story

Boogie Legacy was founded in 2020 by a collective of music historians, audio engineers, and passionate blues enthusiasts who recognized the need to preserve and celebrate John Lee Hooker's monumental contributions to American music. Frustrated by the poor audio quality of existing recordings and the lack of accessible educational resources, our founders set out to create a comprehensive platform that would honor Hooker's legacy while making his music and knowledge available to a global audience. What started as a passion project has evolved into a world-class digital archive and educational platform, featuring the most comprehensive collection of meticulously remastered recordings, immersive virtual experiences, and expert-curated educational content dedicated to this blues legend.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to preserve, celebrate, and share the authentic legacy of John Lee Hooker with the world. We believe in the transformative power of music and its ability to connect people across generations and cultures. We're committed to maintaining the highest standards of audio quality, historical accuracy, and educational excellence in everything we do. We value authenticity above all elseevery recording has been carefully restored using the latest technology while maintaining the integrity of the original performances. We're dedicated to making this content accessible to everyone, from casual music lovers to professional musicians and scholars. Through our work, we honor John Lee Hooker's pioneering spirit and ensure that his revolutionary contributions to blues music continue to inspire and influence artists and audiences for generations to come.
